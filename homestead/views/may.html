Before and After

May 13

Today we started clearing the dirt and debris from the cement area.

We also started trimming back the grass for the garden area, but the battery for the trimmer didn't last very long. We are going to go back with Matty's gas powered trimmer which will hopefully have more strength.

Left: We found a lil' frog behind the tall grass of the cement area. He was living in a hole from the rotted wood of the fence. Looks like our tadpoles will be just fine when they grow up!
Right: The first pile of JUNK we scraped up off the cement. The dirt and roots were surprisingly thick in the north-west corner.


May 14

The last of the mess in the cement area was cleared today! We removed the last of the fence boards, the boats, and moved the cart out of the way to clear the rest of the dirt. We also trimmed back the vines from over the wall.

And we took some fine as hell pictures in our new fermin outfits

AND WE FOUND ANOTHER BABY FROG


May 15

Today we worked back the long grass some more using the gas trimmers, and cleaned up more of the cement area. We also bought some skip bags to collect the grass clippings and extra dirt.


May 16

We pulled up so many damn tires, lengths of barbed wire, and plastic from the garden area today. It was all buried underneath dirt and grass that we're guessing grew over it after it was all left for who knows how long

Nick worked back more of the grass in the garden area, but we had to stop pretty often when we found more barbed wire, lots of rope and string, and what looks like a boat trailer. This is gonna take a lot of work to get the trailer out before we can fully dig the ground in this area.

Hayley cleaned up outside the garden area, and we laid out the tires we found. We are thinking of laying them out along the fence, and maybe using them as planters for some flowers to pretty up the outside area.

We had a classic "put down the shovel and find treasure" moment today. Hayley slapped down the shovel while we were clearing grass and we heard a big thump. We dug up what looks to be either a well or a sewage source. Either way a really cool find.
We also found another frog today! It was right on Hayleys heels, and just a lil' bugger.
